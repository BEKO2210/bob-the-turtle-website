Bob's Diet

As a turtle, I have a varied diet that includes both plant matter and small animals. Here are some of my favorite foods:

  • Leafy greens (lettuce, spinach, kale)
  • Vegetables (carrots, squash, bell peppers)
  • Fruits (apples, berries, melons)
  • Insects (crickets, mealworms)
  • Small fish and aquatic creatures

Remember, different turtle species have different dietary needs. Always consult with a reptile veterinarian or expert to ensure you're providing the right diet for your specific turtle friend!
